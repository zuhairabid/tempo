About Us

Welcome to the world of Jwelery

Rita's Colllection

Here at K1 Jewelers, we provide all types of jewelry, jewelry repairs, and custom design works for everyone. We work with special orders including custom designs of pendents, necklaces, rings, earrings, bracelets, and watches. We have a vast variety of items and merchandise jewelry in our showroom. We also buy gold and resale items. We provide appraisals and engagement/wedding bands and rings. Any customers needing jewelry repairs, watch batteries, eye-glasses repair, and resizing jewelry can be all done here at K1.

We have been in business for the past 30 years in the East Cobb, Georgia area. We provide excellent service and care of our customer's jewelry and merchandise. We are passionate in what we do and love the work we put in to give you the most satisfying piece of joy. Come by and get something special for your friends, family, and loved ones! We are your friendly neighbor Jeweler!
